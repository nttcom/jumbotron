What is the HTML5 JUMBOTRON ?

This app allows the audience to participate on the big screen and liven up events.

Spice up your events with no special equipment required. All you need is a smartphone browser! We have succeeded in showing 150 faces on the big screen in HTML5 Conference 2013.

This application uses SkyWay, an easy to use framework for WebRTC.
